About Elite Custom Homes

Building exceptional homes with unmatched craftsmanship since 2003

Our Story

Founded in 2003 by brothers Michael and David Anderson, Elite Custom Homes began as a small family operation with a simple mission: to build homes of exceptional quality that stand the test of time. What started as a passion project has grown into one of the region's most respected custom home builders.

Over the past two decades, we've constructed more than 300 custom homes, each one unique and tailored to our clients' specific needs and lifestyles. Our commitment to quality has never wavered, and we continue to uphold the same standards of excellence that defined our first projects.

Today, Elite Custom Homes is led by a team of dedicated professionals who share the founders' vision of creating homes that are not just structures, but sanctuaries where families can thrive and create lasting memories.

Community Involvement

At Elite Custom Homes, we believe in giving back to the communities that have supported our growth. We're proud to partner with local organizations and initiatives that make our region a better place to live.

Our community involvement includes:

  • Sponsoring local youth sports teams and educational programs
  • Participating in Habitat for Humanity builds
